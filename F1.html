Formula 1 Racers

PIERRE GASLEY

Alpine Team

Pierre Jean-Jacques Gasly is a French racing driver who competes in Formula One for Alpine. Gasly won the 2020 Italian Grand Prix with AlphaTauri.As of the 2025 Singapore Grand Prix, Gasly has achieved one race win, three fastest laps and five podiums in Formula One. Gasly is contracted to remain at Alpine until at least the end of the 2028 season.

Oscar Piastri

McLaren (Mercedes engines)

Australian, relatively young, quite impressive in recent seasons. Known for strong qualifying and good racecraft, already getting solid results with McLaren.

Lando Norris

McLaren (Mercedes engines)

Fan favorite for his humor and talent. One of the most naturally gifted racers, known for late braking and strong pace. Leads McLaren’s charge after years of steady improvement.

Charles Leclerc

Ferrari

High speed, sometimes hampered by reliability/issues or car performance. Very popular, strong in qualifying, has won multiple races, considered one of the top talents of his generation.

Lewis Hamilton

Ferrari

One of the most successful F1 drivers ever. Has won many titles with Mercedes, and in 2025 switched teams to Ferrari. Known for his qualifying speed, racecraft, resilience.

Max Verstappen

Red Bull Racing

A multiple-time world champion, known for aggressive racing style, strong race craft, and consistency. He became F1’s youngest winner in 2016 and has since been a benchmark for performance in recent seasons.

George Russell

Mercedes

A driver who has shown strong pace, consistency, and often outperformed expectations. He has been with Mercedes since before 2025 and is seen as part of their long-term plans.

Kimi Antonelli

Mercedes

A young Italian who progressed very fast through the junior categories. He had strong success in karting, Formula 4, and regional single-seaters. He joined Mercedes’ driver program and for 2025 took the big step into F1, partnering George Russell, replacing Lewis Hamilton.

Alexander Albon

Williams (Mercedes power)

Versatile driver who revived his F1 career with Williams. Praised for defensive driving and precision. Frequently brings the car higher than expected in races.

Isack Hadjar

Racing Bulls (RB)

F1 rookie with fierce raw speed. Promoted from Formula 2 after multiple wins. Adaptable and aggressive, though still refining his consistency.

Nico Hülkenberg

Sauber (Kick Sauber, Ferrari power)

Veteran driver respected for qualifying brilliance and technical feedback. A key part of Sauber’s development ahead of Audi’s 2026 entry.

Fernando Alonso

Aston Martin Aramco Mercedes

Two-time world champion and one of the oldest drivers on the grid. Still incredibly sharp and competitive. Renowned for racecraft, strategy reading, and tire management.

Carlos Sainz

Williams (Mercedes power)

Reliable and analytical. Moved to Williams after leaving Ferrari. Known as “Smooth Operator” for his calculated overtakes and strategic control.

Lance Stroll

Aston Martin Aramco Mercedes

Stable performer with flashes of brilliance, often strong in wet conditions. Backed by Aston’s owner (his father, Lawrence Stroll), he provides solid midfield results.

Liam Lawson

Red Bull Racing

Gritty and composed under pressure. Earned his full-time seat after impressive sub appearances. Smart and patient — expected to become Red Bull’s next big star.

Esteban Ocon

Haas (Ferrari power)

Tough competitor known for his resilience and defensive driving. Former Alpine driver, joining Haas as team leader for their rebuild.

Yuki Tsunoda

Racing Bulls (RB)

Aggressive and quick, Tsunoda was promoted from Red Bull’s junior team after impressive performances. Matured greatly since his debut, bringing sharp qualifying skills and strong adaptability.

Gabriel Bortoleto

Sauber (Kick Sauber, Ferrari power)

Oliver Bearman

Haas (Ferrari power)

Young British talent who debuted as Ferrari’s stand-in in 2024 and impressed. Smooth, confident, and excellent under pressure — one of F1’s most exciting prospects.

Franco Colapinto

Alpine

Young and talented, the first Argentinian in F1 in over two decades. Quick learner with strong qualifying pace, replacing Jack Doohan mid-season in 2025.
